# Capstone-AI-Humanities
A Generative AI framework for Digital Ethnography: Decoding local authenticity and cultural signals in Vietnamese gastronomy reviews.

# The Authentic Gaze: Decoding Gastronomic Heritage
> **Course:** AI-Aided Creative Exploration in Humanities  
> **Author:** Nguyen Ngoc Huyen Tran
> **Matrikelnummer:** 11922332
> **Status:** Project Proposal (Delivery 1)

## 1. Personal Motivation & Context
This project is inspired by my recent journey through Vietnam. As a member of the Vietnamese diaspora born in the West, my goal was to reconnect with my heritage by seeking out "local-approved" culinary experiences. 

During this trip, I realized that platforms like Google Maps often prioritize high-volume, tourist-rated locations. I found myself manually filtering through hundreds of reviews, looking for Vietnamese names and non-translated "Teencode" to find the "real" food. This project aims to automate that humanistic search for authenticity using Generative AI.

## 2. Project Objectives
The objective of this capstone is to leverage Generative AI to:
* **Deconstruct the "Tourist Bubble":** Identify how algorithms favor Westernized preferences over indigenous local expertise.
* **Bridge the Diaspora Gap:** Create a tool that helps "Việt Kiều" (Overseas Vietnamese) navigate their ancestral culture through data.
* **Empower Small Vendors:** Elevate authentic local stalls that may be hidden by lower review counts but carry high cultural value.

## 3. Relevance to Humanistic Inquiry
This project intersects with **Digital Ethnography** and **Gastropolitics**. It explores how we define "authenticity" in a globalized world and how language serves as a gatekeeper for cultural identity. By analyzing the "Tourist Gaze" vs. the "Local Voice," the project investigates the sociological impact of digital platforms on local food cultures.

## 4. AI Techniques to be Employed
To move beyond simple keyword searches, the project will use:
* **Multilingual LLM Analysis:** Using models like GPT-4 or Gemini to analyze reviews in their original Vietnamese, specifically looking for cultural markers and regional slang (e.g., distinguishing between "ngon" and more colloquial local praise).
* **Sentiment Weighting:** Categorizing reviews based on values. Instead of just "stars," the AI will weigh reviews that focus on broth depth and traditional technique higher than those focusing on English-speaking staff or décor.
* **Zero-Shot Persona Tagging:** Classifying reviewers into "Cultural Insiders" vs. "Casual Travelers" based on linguistic nuance and descriptive detail.

## 5. Expected Outcomes
* **The "Authenticity Index" Prototype:** A framework that re-ranks locations based on a "Local-First" metric.
* **A Methodology Paper:** Documenting how GenAI can be used for cultural preservation and ethnographic research.
* **Creative Reflection:** An analysis of the ethics of using AI to "curate" heritage and the potential for technology to aid in cultural reclamation.
